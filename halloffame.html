Welcome to Carleigh's Pizza Hall of Fame!

Carleigh’s Pizza Hall of Fame is a celebration of the finest plain slices that have achieved perfection. This exclusive collection showcases pizzas that shine through their simplicity—where the balance of a golden crust, flavorful sauce, and perfectly melted cheese creates an unforgettable experience. No frills, no extras—just plain slices that stand out as true masterpieces in the art of pizza-making. If you believe greatness lies in the basics, this is your ultimate guide to the best plain slices around.

#1: Champion Pizza

17 Cleveland Pl, New York, NY 10012

Champion Pizza in Soho, NYC, takes the top spot in Carleigh’s Pizza Hall of Fame, and for good reason. The perfectly charred, thin, and crispy crust is the ideal foundation for its tangy tomato sauce and gooey cheese that blend together effortlessly. Each bite is a satisfying balance of crunch and flavor, making it a standout among New York’s pizza scene. Champion delivers a quintessential slice that embodies everything great about classic NYC pizza, earning its rightful place at the number one spot in the Hall of Fame.

#2: Fratelli's Pizza

202 Ctr, 1 Heritage Hills, Somers, NY 10589

Fratelli’s Pizza in Somers, NY, is an exceptional pizzeria that takes the second spot in Carleigh’s Pizza Hall of Fame. With a perfectly crisp crust and a harmonious balance of tangy sauce and fresh mozzarella, each slice is a testament to their commitment to quality. The flavor profile is classic but elevated, making it a standout in the region. The atmosphere is warm and welcoming, with a family-friendly vibe, perfect for a casual meal or a quick bite. Fratelli’s serves up a pizza that’s both satisfying and delicious, and it’s clear why this place is a local favorite!

#3: 99 Cent Fresh Pizza

473 Lexington Ave, New York, NY 10017

99 Cent Fresh Pizza at 473 Lexington Ave, NYC, takes the third spot in Carleigh’s Pizza Hall of Fame. For a quick and budget-friendly slice, it delivers surprisingly good quality. The thin crust is crisp and holds up well under the generous layer of mozzarella, while the tomato sauce offers a tangy punch. It’s a no-frills option, but what it lacks in ambiance, it more than makes up for in value and consistency. If you’re in the mood for a satisfying slice without breaking the bank, this spot definitely earns its place in the Hall of Fame.
